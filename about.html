MB TRI LOBE SERIES BLOWERS

MB offers a wide range of positive displacement, three lobe blowers for use in compressions and vacuum application for air and other natural gases. The MB TRI LOBE series can deliver pressures upto 1 kg/cm2 with flow rate from 15 m3/h to 11000 m3/h. The blowers for high section pressure are with mechanical seal and pressurized lubrication and other design changes.
