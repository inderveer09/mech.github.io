Due to large setups we offer best in class product with best prices.

MB TRI LOBE SERIES BLOWERS

MB offers a wide range of three lobe blowers for use in compressions and vacuum application for air. The TRI LOBE series can deliver pressures upto 1 kg/cm2 with flow rate from 15 m3/h to 11000 m3/h.

ROTORS

The rotors are precision machined and dynamically balanced to grade 6.3 of ISO-1940/ but mostly to a very finegrade of 2.5 of ISO-1940.

BEARINGS

The bearings are heavy duty ball and roller type with load capacity suited for working conditions, stable performance and long service life.

TIMING GEARS

These drive the rotors in synchronized motion and help maintain clearances between matching lobes. The gears are out of case hardened alloy steel, hardened and ground to high accuracy.

LUBRICATION

In order to ensure ideal lubrication and excellent durability, the bearings located on either end are splash oil lubricated. The forced lubrication (L) is provided for high pressure blowers and on request.

SHAFT - SEALING

The sealing system consist of labyrinth seals, oil thrower discs with a neutral chamber. The compression chamber is thus effectively sealed from the oil chamber and media conveyed is free from oil contamination.
